All,

From yesterday, you have access to a faster, more reliable way to query Aurora’s data. Here’s what you need to know. 

Lakehouse is the future
The Lakehouse combines the best principles of data lakes and data warehouses to provide a platform that will scale with Aurora’s growing data needs. Over the next few weeks all data will be made available in the Lakehouse and, eventually, we will deprecate the old Data Warehouse.

Use Lakehouse for Half-Hourly Plant
Half-Hourly Plant is the first table available in the Lakehouse, because it is the highest-volume table. We recommend that you now use Lakehouse to query half-hourly plant (instructions here).  

The table remains available in Data Warehouse but queries for half-hourly plant will be slow, and are not advised.

Deprecating Data Warehouse
In July all data will be available in the Lakehouse, and we’d like to be able to substantially downsize the old Data Warehouse by September. We’ll share a more detailed timeline in the next couple of weeks - be in touch if you have immediate concerns.
